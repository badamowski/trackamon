Track 'Em All!

Can't find the 'mon on your Sightings list? Track 'Em All is here to help pin point where it is!

Press the SIGHTING button whenever the 'mon is in your Sightings list. This will begin tracking the 'mon by displaying the green area on the map where the 'mon is. You can add more SIGHTING locations to help narrow down the green area.
Press the DISAPPEARED button whenever the 'mon disppears from your Sightings list. This will also help narrow down the green area. If the 'mon disappears you are around 200 meters (650 feet) from the 'mon. So make sure to hurry back to the green area on the map.
Once you have caught the 'mon or ready to start tracking something new, use the CLEAR button to start over.
Use the (Current Location) button to re-draw your position on the map without adding a SIGHTING or DISAPPEARED point. This will also re-draw all the green area in case the map gets a little messed up while hunting.


Remember that 'mons only spawn for so long so be quick and use this tool wisely!

Good luck!!
