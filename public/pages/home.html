Track 'Em All!

Can't find the 'mon on your Sightings list? Track 'Em All is here to help pin point where it is!

When a 'mon appears in your "Sightings" list, flip over to this app and click the SIGHTING button. Flip back to your game, and continue walking. If the 'mon disappears, then come to our app again and click the DISAPPEARED button. If the 'mon is still on your screen you can continue clicking the SIGHTING button to narrow down where they are.

The more bold green the portion of the map will be where the 'mon is most likely at. If the portion is red or yellow then the 'mon is definitely not there, and you should move toward the green areas.

Remember that 'mons only spawn for so long so be quick and use this tool wisely!

Good luck!!
