Track 'Em All!

Can't find the 'mon on your Sightings list? Track 'Em All is here to help pin point where it is!

When a 'mon appears in your "Sightings" list, flip over to Track 'Em All and click the SIGHTING button. Flip back to your game, and continue walking. If the 'mon disappears, then come to Track 'Em All again and click the DISAPPEARED button. If the 'mon is still on your screen you can continue clicking the SIGHTING button to narrow down where they are.

The 'mon you are tracking will be in the green portion of the map. As you add extra SIGHTING and DISAPPEARED points Track 'Em All will narrow down the green areas to help guide you to that 'mon.

Remember that 'mons only spawn for so long so be quick and use this tool wisely!

Good luck!!
